Find the Outlaw! – Odd One Out Puzzle Game for iPhone

Find the Outlaw! is a free observation and logic puzzle game for iPhone. Watch a group of characters move and find the character that does not follow the same rule.

What is Find the Outlaw!?

Find the Outlaw! is a casual puzzle game based on observation, movement patterns, and rule recognition. Each stage contains a group of characters, but one or more characters move differently from the others.

How do you play?

  1. Watch the characters move.
  2. Identify the common movement rule.
  3. Tap the character that breaks the rule.
  4. Clear the stage and continue to the next puzzle.

Who is the game for?

The game is designed for casual puzzle players and people who enjoy short observation, pattern-recognition, and brain-training challenges.

Does the game require an account?

No. Account registration and login are not required. Game progress is stored locally on the device.

Does the game contain advertising?

Yes. The game may display banner and interstitial advertisements through Google AdMob. Advertising is not required to access the core gameplay.

Where can I download the game?

Find the Outlaw! is available for iPhone on the Apple App Store.
